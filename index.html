About

I'm a software engineer passionate about building maintainable and efficient software solutions. With a strong focus on backend development, I believe that simplicity is the key to creating clear, reliable systems that are easy to evolve over time.

I approach software design with methodologies like Domain-Driven Design (DDD), emphasizing the importance of understanding the business domain and collaborating closely with industry experts. Beyond backend development, I bring full-stack expertise to the table, including DevOps skills and frontend development experience, making me a well-rounded developer ready to tackle diverse challenges.

Outside of coding, I’m a music enthusiast and a guitarist, as well as a football fan and a lover of oriental cuisine. These passions fuel my creativity and keep me curious about the world.
